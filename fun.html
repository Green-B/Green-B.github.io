Gnomes

I crochet sometimes to keep my hands busy. Some time ago, I was asked to make one, then another and another as friends would see and request their own. By now I've made well over 100 crochet gnomes who accompany me on my travels. They have their own Instagram, Bluesky, Mastodon, and YouTube accounts. Working with the gnomes has led me to learn much about photography, photo editing, and video editing. I use GIMP for photo editing and Shotcut for video editing.

Puns & jokes

Word jokes are my favorite jokes. Whatever my attention is on, I will make puns about it, from theoretical physics to international diplomacy and from woodworking to games. My puns were originally posted to Facebook, but this account is now archival and future puns will be posted to Bluesky.
